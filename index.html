Our Educational Philosophy

At Apple Tree Tots, we believe that the early years are fundamental in shaping a child's lifelong love of learning. Our approach combines the time-tested methods of AMI Montessori with contemporary insights from the HEI Holistic Early Intervention framework.

We've created an environment where children feel safe to explore, confident to question, and empowered to discover their unique capabilities. Our classrooms are carefully prepared to encourage independence while fostering a sense of community.

Our educators are not just teachers but guides who observe each child's individual needs and interests, providing appropriate materials and challenges at just the right moment in their development.
