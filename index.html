About Apple Tree Tots

Founded with a vision to create a nurturing space where children can grow, explore, and discover their potential through the perfect blend of structured learning and creative freedom.

Our experienced educators are passionate about early childhood development and are trained in both AMI Montessori and HEI Method approaches.

We believe every child is unique and deserves an education that celebrates their individuality while building strong foundations for lifelong learning.
