Our Educational Philosophy

At Apple Tree Tots, we believe that the early years are fundamental in shaping a child's lifelong love of learning. Our approach combines the time-tested methods of AMI Montessori with contemporary insights from the HEI Holistic Early Intervention framework.

We've created a nurturing environment where children feel safe to explore, confident to question, and empowered to discover their unique capabilities. Our light-filled classrooms are carefully prepared to encourage independence while fostering a sense of community and belonging.

Our educators are not just teachers but compassionate guides who observe each child's individual needs and interests, providing appropriate materials and challenges at just the right moment in their developmental journey.

Our Educational Programs

Seedlings Program

Ages 18 months - 3 years

Building independence and foundational skills through purposeful work and play in a nurturing environment

  • Practical life activities & self-care skills
  • Language development & vocabulary building
  • Sensorial exploration of textures, sounds, colors
  • Social skills development & emotional regulation
  • Fine & gross motor skill refinement

Primary Program

Ages 3 - 6 years

Comprehensive Montessori education fostering academic excellence, personal growth, and social development

  • Mathematics & numeracy with concrete materials
  • Language arts, phonics, and early literacy
  • Cultural studies & global awareness
  • Scientific exploration & nature studies
  • Creative expression through art and music
