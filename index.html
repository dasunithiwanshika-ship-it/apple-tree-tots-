OUR CURRICULUM

AMI MONTESSORI + HEI METHOD

AMI MONTESSORI

Developed by Dr. Maria Montessori and internationally recognized by AMI

Association Montessori Internationale

  • Focuses on hands-on learning, independence, and self-paced discovery
  • Helps children develop practical life skills, sensory awareness, and critical thinking

HEI METHOD (HOLISTIC EARLY INTERVENTION)

  • A nature-based, holistic approach to learning
  • Encourages emotional, social, and creative development
  • Uses play, storytelling, art, and outdoor exploration to nurture well-rounded, confident children
