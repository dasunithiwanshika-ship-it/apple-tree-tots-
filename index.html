About Apple Tree Tots

Founded with a vision to create a nurturing space where children can grow, explore, and discover their potential through the perfect blend of structured learning and creative freedom.

Our experienced educators are passionate about early childhood development and are trained in both AMI Montessori and HEI Method approaches.

We believe every child is unique and deserves an education that celebrates their individuality while building strong foundations for lifelong learning.

Our Curriculum

Combining proven Montessori methods with innovative holistic approaches

AMI MONTESSORI + HEI METHOD

AMI MONTESSORI

Developed by Dr. Maria Montessori and internationally recognized by AMI

Association Montessori Internationale

  • Focuses on hands-on learning, independence, and self-paced discovery
  • Helps children develop practical life skills, sensory awareness, and critical thinking
  • Child-centered environment with specially designed learning materials
  • Mixed-age classrooms that foster peer learning and social development

HEI METHOD (HOLISTIC EARLY INTERVENTION)

  • A nature-based, holistic approach to learning
  • Encourages emotional, social, and creative development
  • Uses play, storytelling, art, and outdoor exploration to nurture well-rounded, confident children
  • Integrates mindfulness and emotional intelligence practices
  • Focuses on building resilience and positive self-identity
